See KeelWorks events in your personal calendar

  1. Open KeelWorks Google Calendar (@keelworks.org).
  2. Under My calendars, hover your name → ⋮ → Settings and sharing.
  3. Share with your personal Gmail → choose permission → Send.
  4. Accept the invite from your personal calendar.
Why this matters

View KeelWorks meetings alongside personal events—no juggling multiple calendars.
